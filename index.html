Pusheen Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Your goal is to match your total to the random number.

Clicking on a Pusheen will give you points, but each Pusheen contains a different number of points -- and these point values are randomly generated at the start of each game. You won't know the values until you click on each Pusheen.

You must reach the random number through a combination of Pusheen Points.
